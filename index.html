About Me

Education

I am currently enrolled in the Computer Engineering program at the University of Michigan-Dearborn in Dearborn, Michigan. I am at Senior-standing based on credit amount, but am not projected to offically obtain my Bachelor of Science in Engineering until May 2024.

I graduated from Woodhaven High School in Flat Rock, Michigan in June of 2019. From January of 2017 to April 2019, I was dual-enrolled at Wayne County Community College in Taylor, Michigan as a part of their Early/Middle College program. After graduating high school, I stayed at WCCCD for the following school year and graduated with an Associate of Arts degree in June 2020. I then transferred the following fall semester to Oakland University in Rochester, Michigan. While at Oakland, I was apart of their Honors College program and an active member of the Delta Tau chapter of the Alpha Sigma Tau National Sorority. I realized fairly quickly that the quality of my education could be higher and more beneficial to me at a different institution, so I transferred to the University of Michigan-Dearborn for the fall 2021 semester.
